About Us

We are committed to providing high-quality education that nurtures creativity, curiosity, and lifelong learning. Our goal is to empower students to become innovative leaders of tomorrow, equipped with knowledge, skills, and values to make a meaningful impact in their communities.

Our Mission

  • Students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment.
  • Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner.
  • Administrators and staff ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning.
  • Family, community, and stakeholders actively engage in developing lifelong learners.

Our Vision

We envision nurturing Filipinos who passionately love their country, possessing values and competencies that enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to nation-building. As a learner-centered public institution, we continuously improve to better serve our stakeholders and create transformative educational experiences.

Our Location

Initao National Comprehensive High School
